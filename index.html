Leading the Vision

Where construction
meets creativity

Braw House was founded to bridge the gap between beautiful design and practical delivery. With over a decade of experience across construction management and high-end interiors, our founder Chris Law brings a rare perspective — understanding not just how spaces should look, but how they're built.

From Edinburgh's Georgian townhouses to contemporary commercial spaces, we create interiors defined by intention, craftsmanship, and enduring quality.

About the Studio

How We Work

A refined process

Discovery

Every project begins with understanding — your space, your style, and how you'll live or work within it. We listen deeply to build a foundation of purpose.

Concept

Through moodboards, material palettes, and spatial plans, we translate ideas into atmosphere and narrative — shaping a vision that is both unique and considered.

Detailing

The vision becomes tangible. We refine every element — from 3D visualisation and specification to finishes and furnishings — ensuring precision at every scale.

Delivery

We orchestrate the installation and handover, managing every discipline and detail. Our construction experience ensures the process runs to programme and budget.
